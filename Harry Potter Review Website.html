Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Movie Review

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows is a great film which will have you on the edge of you seat.

I really like Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows because there is just so much actions going on.

Anyway, I love the Harry Potter series. I am obsessed. I especially love the Battle of Hogwarts.

Spolier Alert! Snape is killed my Voldemort!

The Battle of the Seven Potters was great too. It was funny how many Harry's there were. But sad when Hedwig died.

The Battle of Hagwarts happens in part 2 and that is when Voldemort's Army and Dumbledore's Army fight. There were trolls, werewolves, wizards, stone people, etc.


The part when Harry got the sword definitly had me on the edge of my seat. I thought he was going to be stuck and drown. But then Ron rescued him even though they were mad at each other.

And when Harry rescued Draco in the Room of Requirement was great. I know they were enemies, but Harry made a good choice.

The Battle of Hogwarts was definitly my favorite part. With all the spells, action, running around, and not to mention a little romance.

But the ending was so happy, I love the ending of the movie. Taking their kids to the Hogwarts Express.
